# Banana Banana Bread
A simple banana bread. The more ripe the bananas, the better.

## info
*   **Servings:** 12
*   **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Cook Time:** 60 minutes

## ingredients
### Dry
*   2 cups all-purpose flour
*   1 tbsp. baking soda
*   1/4 tsp. salt

### Wet
*   1/2 cup butter, melted
*   3/4 cup brown sugar
*   2 eggs, beaten
*   2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

![Banana Bread](images/bananabread.jpg)

## steps
1.  Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2.  In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
3.  In a separate bowl, mix together melted butter and brown sugar until smooth. Stir in beaten eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
4.  Pour the banana mixture into the flour mixture and stir until just moistened — do not overmix.
5.  Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
6.  Bake for 60–65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7.  Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
